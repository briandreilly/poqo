# poqo Demo: Factual Claim

---

## Question

Is exercise important for everyone?

---

## Candidate Responses

### Response A
Yes, exercise is important for everyone.

### Response B
Exercise is generally beneficial for most people, but the type, intensity, and frequency should be adjusted based on age, health conditions, and physical ability.

---

## poqo Judgment

### Response A → ❌ Rejected

Reason:
- Overgeneralized claim
- No conditions or boundaries
- Not sufficiently defined

---

### Response B → ✅ Accepted

Reason:
- Uses qualified language ("generally", "most")
- Introduces constraints and variables
- Defines how the claim applies
- Meets sufficiently defined standard

---

## Result

Response B is selected as valid.
