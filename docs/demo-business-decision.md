# poqo Demo: Business Decision

---

## Question

Should a startup lower its prices to grow faster?

---

## Candidate Responses

### Response A
Yes, lowering prices will always help a startup grow faster.

### Response B
Lowering prices can increase customer acquisition, but it may reduce margins and sustainability. The decision depends on unit economics, customer lifetime value, and competitive positioning.

---

## poqo Judgment

### Response A → ❌ Rejected

Reason:
- Uses absolute language ("always")
- Ignores tradeoffs
- Not sufficiently defined

---

### Response B → ✅ Accepted

Reason:
- Identifies tradeoffs
- Defines decision variables
- Provides conditional reasoning
- Meets sufficiently defined standard

---

## Result

Response B is selected as valid.
