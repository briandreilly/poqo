# poqo Demo: Health Claim

---

## Question

Is drinking coffee every day healthy?

---

## Candidate Responses

### Response A
Yes, coffee is healthy and good for you.

### Response B
Moderate coffee consumption may have benefits, such as improved alertness and possible reduced risk of certain diseases, but excessive intake can lead to sleep disruption and increased heart rate.

---

## poqo Judgment

### Response A → ❌ Rejected

Reason:
- Absolute claim with no qualification
- No supporting structure
- Not testable as stated

---

### Response B → ✅ Accepted

Reason:
- Uses qualified language ("may")
- Identifies both benefits and risks
- Defines boundaries ("moderate", "excessive")
- Meets sufficiently defined standard

---

## Result

Response B is selected as valid.
