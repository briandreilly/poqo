# poqo Demo: Attitude Example — Social Media

This example shows how poqo keeps the same reasoning standard while changing response attitude.

---

## Input

Social media is toxic.

---

## normal

That’s a broad claim. What specifically do you mean by “toxic”?

---

## challenge

“Toxic” is vague. What definition are you using, and what evidence supports it?

---

## difficult

That’s an unsupported generalization. Narrow it or provide a concrete example.

---

## What stays the same

All three responses reject the claim as too broad and insufficiently defined.

---

## What changes

Only the response attitude changes:
- `normal` = cooperative
- `challenge` = stronger pushback
- `difficult` = lowest tolerance
